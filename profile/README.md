# Camunda

[Camunda](https://camunda.com) offers a scalable platform for process orchestration and automation, used by developers and enterprises worldwide. Design and run end-to-end processes that combine deterministic workflows with dynamic agentic orchestration—spanning people, systems, and AI agents—on our SaaS, in your cloud, or on-premises.

## Camunda 8

[Camunda 8](https://camunda.com/platform/) is composed of the following components:

* [Orchestration Cluster](https://github.com/camunda/camunda) | ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/orchestration-cluster/)): the Orchestration Cluster is the core component of Camunda 8, powering the automation and orchestration of processes. It includes:
  * Zeebe ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/zeebe/zeebe-overview/)) - The cloud-native process engine of Camunda 8.
  * Tasklist ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/tasklist/introduction-to-tasklist/)) - Complete tasks that require human input.
  * Operate ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/operate/operate-introduction/)) - Manage, monitor, and troubleshoot your processes.
  * Identity ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/identity/identity-introduction/)) - Manage integrated authentication and authorization.
  * Optimize ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/optimize/what-is-optimize/)) - Improve your processes by identifying constraints in your system.
* (The following components are part of Camunda 8, but are delivered as individual components / via individual repositories):
  * [Connectors](https://github.com/camunda/connectors) | ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/connectors/introduction-to-connectors/)) - Easily connect processes to external systems, applications, and data using reusable building blocks.
  * Console ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/console/introduction-to-console/)) - Configure and deploy clusters in SaaS with Console.
  * Web Modeler ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/modeler/web-modeler/)) - Collaboratively design executable BPMN, DMN, and Forms in your web browser.
  * [Desktop Modeler](https://github.com/camunda/camunda-modeler) | ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/components/modeler/desktop-modeler/)) - Design executable BPMN, DMN, Forms, and RPA scripts on your local machine.
  * [Camunda HELM Charts](https://github.com/camunda/camunda-platform-helm) | ([docs](https://docs.camunda.io/docs/self-managed/about-self-managed/)) - HELM Charts to set up Camunda 8 Self-Managed in your k8s environment.
